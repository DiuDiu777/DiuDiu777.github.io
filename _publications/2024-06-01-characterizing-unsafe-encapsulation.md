An empirical study of how "interior unsafe" is achieved in practice in real-world Rust projects. We identify best practices for encapsulating unsafe code and uncover common issues including forgetting to specify safety requirements for unsafe constructors, and ignoring literal constructors that can bypass safety requirements.
